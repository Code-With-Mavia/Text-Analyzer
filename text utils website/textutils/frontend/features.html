Features of TextUtils Pro

  • Remove Punctuation: Quickly clean up text by eliminating all punctuation marks for better readability.
  • Case Conversion: Convert text to uppercase or lowercase instantly to standardize documents.
  • Newline & Extra Space Removal: Streamline text formatting by removing unnecessary line breaks and extra spaces.
  • Character & Sentence Counting: Get real-time analytics of text, including total characters and sentences for precise editing.
  • Clean, Responsive Design: Built with Bootstrap 5 to provide an intuitive and professional user interface.
